Our World

Our world is in crisis. It’s political, economic, environmental, and spiritual.

Everyone is asking why but few of us are satisfied with the answers our politicians or much of the mainstream media are giving us.

The Rules works to make sense of these crises and contribute to the transition toward a more just and thriving world. We connect dots to show how seemingly separate issues are interconnected. We reveal and critique the rules of the current system, and shine a light on beautiful alternatives that work for all and honour Mother Earth.

Portrait inspired by Standing Rock

As writer Arundhati Roy says: “Another world is not only possible, she is on her way. On a quiet day, I can hear her breathing.”
